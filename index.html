About Mood Swings

Mood Swings helps you find the perfect music for how you're feeling — all from the YouTube library.

  • Pick a Mood — Click any mood card (Happy, Sad, Romantic, Motivated, Energetic) to open a YouTube search of songs matching that vibe in a new tab.
  • Take the Quiz — Not sure what you're in the mood for? Answer 3 quick questions and get a personalized mood suggestion.
  • Player Bar — Once a mood is playing, use the footer bar to skip between moods (prev/next), favorite your current mood, or minimize the bar into a compact pill.
  • Favorites — Click the heart icon in the player bar to save moods you love. Your favorites are remembered on return visits.
  • Search — Type any song or artist name in the search bar and press Enter to search YouTube directly.
  • Sign In — Click the profile icon to sign in (demo). Your name appears in the profile dropdown after signing in.
  • Persistent State — Your last-played mood and favorites are saved automatically and restored when you come back.

All music playback happens on YouTube in a new tab. Close the tab or click the active mood again to switch.
